1. Description

Dr.Fly is an app that enables you to buy your medecines online and be delivered at home by a drone: the Dromee. The Dromee arrives whenever you want, so you don't need to go to the drugstore to buy you medecines. You just need to scan your prescription on the app and the Dromee delivers within few minutes. If you live far from the drugstore, are busy or just lazy, Dr.Fly is THE app for you.
